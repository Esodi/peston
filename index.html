Chairman's Message

Welcome to the official Peston Group website!

As we unveil this new digital platform, we are excited to share our journey of growth, innovation, and transformation. This website represents more than just an online presence; it embodies the heart of Peston Group's vision to make a significant impact in the industries we serve. Here, you will find the latest updates on our initiatives, projects, and the milestones we've achieved.

Since our foundation in 2016, Peston Group has been driven by a singular mission: to create value across various sectors while maintaining a strong commitment to quality, sustainability, and ethical practices. What began as an agribusiness trading house has evolved into a dynamic, multi-faceted conglomerate with a diverse portfolio in agribusiness, agro-processing, real estate, branding, marketing, and entertainment. Each of these areas holds the potential to reshape industries and improve lives, and we are proud of the progress we've made.

As we expand both within Tanzania and globally through digital platforms, we remain focused on empowering communities, nurturing talent, and elevating the standards of service in every industry we touch. We are committed to creating long-term value for our clients, partners, and all those we serve.

This journey would not have been possible without the unwavering trust and collaboration of our valued clients, partners, and dedicated employees. I extend my deepest gratitude to all who have contributed to our success, and I look forward to continuing this journey together as we build a future that is prosperous, inclusive, and impactful.

Thank you for your support, and we are excited for what lies ahead.

Petro Estomih Nkya

Chairman, Peston Group
