Company Profile

Explore our journey, mission, and values that drive Peston Group to deliver exceptional results in entertainment and real estate marketing.

REAL ESTATE

Peston Real Estate Limited is a legally incorporated entity under Cap 212 of the Laws of the United Republic of Tanzania. Established in August 2022, it operates as a subsidiary of the esteemed Peston Group of Companies, specializing in the Real Estate Industry. Our company boasts a team of seasoned professionals in the field, whose collective expertise, integrity, and dedication serve as our cornerstone. We prioritize the cultivation of enduring relationships, both internally and externally.

VISION

Our vision is to emerge as a premier Pan-African Real Estate firm, renowned for delivering exemplary services across all client tiers.

MISSION

We are committed to revolutionizing and modernizing the real estate landscape, offering tailored solutions that swiftly address and fulfil the diverse needs of our clientele with precision and excellence.
