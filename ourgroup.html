Our History

2016: Peston Group was founded by Petro Nkya, starting with the establishment of Peston Enterprises Limited, focusing on agribusiness and trading.

2022: Peston Real Estate Limited and Peston Entertainment Limited were founded, expanding the Group's operations into real estate and marketing & entertainment sectors.

2025: Peston Group continues to innovate, embracing technology and sustainability, with a diverse portfolio of companies driving growth across various industries.

Our Mission & Vision

Vision:

To be a leading Pan-African conglomerate, setting the benchmark for excellence, innovation, and sustainability across industries, while improving lives with affordable, high-quality products and services.

Mission:

To empower communities and businesses by delivering world-class products and services that exceed expectations. We focus on continuous innovation, leveraging technology to create lasting value and ensuring sustainable growth for all stakeholders.

Our Core Values

At Peston Group, our core values are the foundation of everything we do, guiding our operations and shaping our culture.

Integrity and Transparency:

We conduct our business with the highest level of integrity and transparency, ensuring that honesty and ethical standards govern all our interactions.

Accountability:

We take responsibility for our actions and decisions, ensuring that we are always accountable to our clients, partners, and stakeholders.

Teamwork:

We believe in the power of collaboration. By fostering a culture of teamwork, we ensure that every individual contributes their expertise to achieve our collective goals.

Commitment:

Our dedication to delivering on promises and exceeding expectations drives us. We are committed to excellence, continuously improving and innovating in all areas of our business.

Professionalism and Excellence:

We maintain the highest standards of professionalism in every aspect of our operations, striving for excellence in service delivery, client satisfaction, and industry leadership.

Customer Focus:

Our clients are at the heart of everything we do. We listen to their needs, anticipate their preferences, and tailor our services to ensure their success and satisfaction.
