AGENCY & ENTERTAINMENT

Peston Entertainment Limited is an innovative full-service company specializing in Branding, Communications, Marketing, and Entertainment, headquartered in Dar es Salaam, Tanzania. Our mission is to elevate brands through ingenious ideas, digital strategies, and market-ready innovations. With a comprehensive portfolio that includes Visual and Audio Productions, a cutting-edge creative marketing agency, and access to high-end technology facilities, we create endless opportunities for brands to strategically engage with their audience through meticulously planned communications.
